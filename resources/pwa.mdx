Install Tembo as a PWA for quick access from your home screen with an app-like experience.

## Installation

1. Open [app.tembo.io](https://app.tembo.io) in your browser
2. Access the share/install menu (address bar icon or browser menu)
3. Select "Add to Home Screen" and confirm

**Benefits:** Quick home screen access, full-screen mode, offline resilience, and push notifications (coming soon).

## Troubleshooting

**Install option not visible?**
- **Chrome Desktop:** Install icon in address bar (computer with down arrow)
- **Safari iOS:** Share button at bottom → scroll to "Add to Home Screen"
- **Chrome Android:** Three-dot menu → "Add to Home Screen" or "Install app"
- **Edge:** Three-dot menu → "Apps" → "Install Tembo"

**Supported browsers:** Chrome/Edge (all platforms), Safari (iOS 14.3+), Firefox (limited).

**PWA not updating?** Remove/uninstall the app, clear cache if needed, then reinstall from your browser.
